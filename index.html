Facts about me

Hi, first of all, I'm just a normal student. I'm a student who isn't wealthy
and comes from a poor family—a family where as soon as one problem is solved,
a new one arises. I'm not particularly smart and I'm often lazy about many
things, but I'm the type of student who, despite being lazy, is very grade-
conscious. I'll do anything for good grades, even if it feels tedious.
To be honest, I dream of getting rich without working too hard. That's why
I chose IT, because I love gadgets, and I thought it would be an advantage
for me. But now that I'm in college, I realize I was wrong—this course is
extremely challenging. However, I think it's also enjoyable. I will continue
to fight and strive hard for my family to repay all the sacrifices they have made for me.
